# DoraHacks Submission

## Project Name

RWA Risk Sentinel

## One-liner

Autonomous AI risk oracle for tokenized real-world assets, publishing verifiable attestations on Casper Testnet.

## Short Description

RWA Risk Sentinel is an agentic AI application for tokenized real-world assets. The agent evaluates off-chain RWA signals such as payment delay, debtor credit quality, macro volatility, collateral drawdown, and negative news. It then computes a deterministic risk score, creates an evidence hash, and publishes a verifiable attestation to a Casper Testnet smart contract.

The goal is to give DeFi protocols and RWA platforms a machine-readable trust layer for AI-generated asset intelligence.
